Hello, I am a physicist. I am mainly interested in condensed matter physics, theoretical physics, and computational physics. My work involves developing computational methods and the corresponding software for solving quantum many-body problems. I also employ these methods and software to investigate the exotic properties of strongly correlated models and realistic correlated materials. Recently, I focus on the ultrafast dynamics in quantum materials.
